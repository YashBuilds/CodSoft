Ratan Tata

A Visionary Who Reformed Indian Industry

About

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and a former chairman of Tata Sons. He led the Tata Group from 1990 to 2012 and again as interim chairman in 2016–17. Under his leadership, Tata Group grew immensely and expanded internationally.

Achievements

  • Padma Bhushan (2000) and Padma Vibhushan (2008) recipient.
  • Responsible for the acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley Tea.
  • Launched Tata Nano, the world’s cheapest car.
  • Supports startups and innovation through Tata Trusts.

Inspiration

He is admired for his humility, vision, and ethical business practices. Ratan Tata remains an inspiration for generations of entrepreneurs and leaders.
